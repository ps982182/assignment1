ABOUT ABES ENGINEERING COLLEGE, GZB

ABES Engineering College, established in the year 2000, is an esteemed institution of Engineering Sciences in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, which has been serving the evergreen demand of ambitious technocrats. It belongs to the Society of Educational Excellence, Ghaziabad, which was founded in 1999 with the mission to provide quality training to the youth in Engineering and Management studies. ABES Engineering College is a self-financed and private Engineering Institution that has been approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE) and is also affiliated to the Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh). 

    Location 
    ABES Engineering College is a part of Campus-1 and is situated at 19th KM Stone, in proximity to national highway 24, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. The campus covers a vast 15.2 acres of land. In terms of accessibility and connectivity, the college is well-connected with public and private transport. Ghaziabad railway station at a distance of 12 kilometers, whereas Indira Gandhi International Airport is around 44 kilometers away from the college. Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Airport is the closest Domestic Airport located 48 kilometers away. Shaheed Sthal metro station is only 10 kilometers away, which makes the college accessible to students living across NCR and NCT regions.

    Admissions 
    ABES Engineering College is open for aspirants looking for undergraduate and postgraduate courses. Students can apply for admissions in the college for undergraduate programmes through UPSEE examinations followed by a counseling session. At the same time, admissions for postgraduate courses can be made through GATE/UPSEE counseling sessions. 
    
    For Undergraduate Courses 
    ●       The candidates have to first qualify for the UPSEE exam to apply for the college.
    
    ●       An online application form is to be filled by the students available on the website of ABES Engineering College.
    
    ●       If a candidate qualifies for admission, he or she has to attend a counseling session held by the college and will be selected on a merit basis. 
    
    For Postgraduate Courses 
    ●       Students have to qualify for the GATE examination for eligibility.
    
    ●       The candidates have to apply for the course via filling an application form on the website of the college.
    
    ●       Qualified candidates have to attend the UPSEE counseling process, and seats will be allotted as per preferences.
    
    ●       For MBA and MCA, seats will be provided via UPSEE counseling.
